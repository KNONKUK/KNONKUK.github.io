Scarred Defiant for Life

This is a zine I made in 2018 about surviving sexual violence emotionally. It has thoughts, images, poems, experiences (there are no scenes of sexual violence but there is a story about harassment). I made this a long time ago and I can't wholeheartedly stand behind everything I say here, but I've decided to publish it unaltered. This is one person's perspective, experience, thoughts. I hope it is of use.

If you would like a physical copy, you are welcome to print it. Download the full size, paginated pdf here: KNONK: Buy me a Coffee. There's an opportunity to tip but you really don't have to.

I will be adding descriptions of the images and reprduce the text below the pages for if you're struggling with small lettering or using a screen reader, but that's not ready yet.
